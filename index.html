Who

I sit at the intersection of software engineering, data science, and capital markets operations — a combination rare enough that I've spent most of my career building things that didn't exist yet. I'm drawn to problems where the answer isn't obvious, the domain is complex, and the solution has to actually work in production.

Right now I'm building Candela at FINBOURNE — an agentic AI platform that gives investment operations teams AI that doesn't just answer questions but executes real workflows against live financial data. Before that, I spent over a decade at Northern Trust progressively moving from operations, to risk, to data science, to leading transformation at the SVP level.

Outside of work I read obsessively, think too much about distributed systems, and occasionally teach data science to people who've never written a line of code.
